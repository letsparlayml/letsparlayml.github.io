About

How to use this page

Start with the projected score

The model is built to predict individual team final scores first. Moneyline, spread, and total leans are all derived from that score projection rather than picked independently.

Use the quick looks for a fast scan

The NBA quick looks section highlights spread and total edges, stronger floor-style prop spots, higher-variance ceiling ideas, and props that sit far above or below a player’s recent baseline.

Open the props lab for deeper analysis

The props lab adds player search, stat filters, L5/L10/L25 hit rates, projected minutes, fair odds, and the main reasons for and against each prop.

How results are graded

The results area tracks moneyline, spread, and total performance for yesterday plus current week, month, and year, both overall and separated by league.
