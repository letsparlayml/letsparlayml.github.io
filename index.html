About

How to use this page

Start with the projected score

The model is built to predict final scores first. Moneyline, spread, and total leans are all derived from that score projection rather than picked independently.

Today vs next day

Today’s board is the playable slate. The next-day section is an early research board. Those games stay at N/A for market lines until reliable numbers are available.

How to read the props table

Model prediction is the expected stat output. Probability is the model-estimated chance of clearing the listed threshold. Confidence is a rough bucket, not a guarantee.

How results are graded

The results table compares the predicted score to the actual final score and grades how the model would have done on moneyline, spread, and total using the market lines available for that game date.
