How it works

Consistent pregame tracking

Spreads on this page are shown from the away-team perspective across every league. Positive means the away team is getting points, pucks, or runs. Current means the latest pregame snapshot. Once a game starts, current becomes the close.

Best away vs best home

The best away spread is the highest away spread seen before start. The best home spread is the lowest away spread seen before start.

Best over vs best under

The best over total is the lowest total seen before start. The best under total is the highest total seen before start.

Model edge

Spread edge uses the model’s away-team spread from the projected scores. Total edge uses the model total. Positive spread edge favors the away side at that number.
